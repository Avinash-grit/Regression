## Ask AIM Chatbot


### Project Summary:
The "ASK AIM Chatbot" project focused on developing a chatbot tailored for vehicle launch program managers to facilitate a better understanding of the program status. Leveraging advanced technologies and a comprehensive pipeline, the chatbot aimed to streamline communication and provide real-time insights into the status of issues, ultimately aiding launch managers in startup meetings.

### Business Context:
The need for an AI-powered chatbot arose from the complex and dynamic nature of vehicle launch programs, where managers often require quick and accurate updates on program status and issues. The chatbot was designed to enhance communication efficiency, provide instant access to relevant information, and support informed decision-making during critical startup meetings.
